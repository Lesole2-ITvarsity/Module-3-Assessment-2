Dalgona

Dalgona coffee, also known as hand beaten coffee,
is a beverage originating from Macau made by whipping equal
parts instant coffee powder,
sugar, and hot water until it becomes creamy
and then adding it to cold or hot milk.

Total time: 5mins
Servings: 1
