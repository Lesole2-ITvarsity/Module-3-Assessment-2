Steamed sea bass

Try this recipe for soy-steamed sea bass.
Serving the whole fish at the table means less prep and it looks really impressive,
whilst cooking en papillote means the fish delicately steams to
perfection without having to stand over it.

Total time: 50mins
Servings: 4
