Steak Tartare

Mix together all the ingredients except the egg with a
good pinch of black pepper and 2 good pinches of Maldon sea salt.
Arrange on a small plate (you can use a ring) and top with the egg yolk
(leave it in a half shell for presentation if you like).
Season the egg yolk and serve.


Total time: 15min
Servings: 1
