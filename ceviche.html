Scallope ceviche

Scallop ceviche is a tangy delicacy where thinly sliced scallops
are marinated in citrus juice with red onions, cilantro, and jalapeños.
Refreshing and elegant,
it's ideal as a light appetizer or main course.

Servings: 3
